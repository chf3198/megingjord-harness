## Summary
Safety **meta-guardrails** over the fleet-dev control loop (Epic #2791 P1-6; Phase-0 #2792 design D10/D11) —
they stop the system gaming its own metrics.

- **Anti-Goodhart** (`fleet-dev-anti-goodhart.js`): captures per-change quality (coverage delta + mutation
  score + test/code complexity ratio) and trips when fleet-development-**share** rises while composite
  **quality** falls over a velocity-relative window (least-squares slope → catches slow drift) → Tier-2
  anneal + **gate the share metric** so G3 can't silently erode G2.
- **Critic-calibration** (`fleet-dev-critic-calibration.js`): tracks the #2797 critic's precision vs
  human-validated escalations, prove-it test-acceptance, and post-refresh verdict drift; each check is
  `ok`/`degraded`/`insufficient-data` (a starved pipeline surfaces as **unknown, never silent-healthy**) →
  anneal + recalibrate on degradation.

Both pure, emit-injectable, fail-safe on thin samples.
