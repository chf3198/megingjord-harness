## Decision

**CONFIRM v3's 72% claim.** Under Anthropic's published rates (write 1.25×, read 0.10×, 5-min ephemeral or 1-h extended):

| Session | Hit rate | Reduction |
|---|---|---|
| 10-call (Sonnet) | 90% | **73.5%** |
| 100-call | 99% | **83.3%** |
| 5-call | 80% | **65.6%** |
| Single write + miss | 0% | -25% (write surcharge only) |

All sessions ≥ v3's 72% target when hit rate ≥80% (HAMR design floor).

## Recommendations to HAMR v3

1. **Use 1-h extended cache** (2.0× write, same 0.10× read) for HAMR's 15–60 min session shape; ephemeral 5-min cache misses on Manager → Collaborator → Admin transitions that exceed 5 min.
2. **Rate-limit bundle-rebuild cadence to ≥5 min** for ephemeral cache amortization — coordinate with DC-1 (signed mailbox arrivals must not trigger immediate re-bundle).
3. **Tool definitions ride in cached prefix**, not the variable tail.
