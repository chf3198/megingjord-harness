## Summary
Upgrades #2797's cross-family critic flaw classifier from a **keyword-only heuristic** to **structured tags**
(Epic #2791 follow-on), closing the residual semantic-gap false-negative gemini flagged when #2797 shipped.

- The `pr-diff` review prompt (`config/fleet-red-team-prompts.json`) now asks critics to append
  `[tags: security,correctness,concurrency,merge-safety] [severity: high|medium|low]` to each finding.
- `scripts/global/fleet-critic-flaw-classifier.js` `classifyFlaw(text)` parses tags **first** — flaw iff a
  flaw-class tag OR `severity: high` (fail-safe: a misspelled tag can't downgrade a high-severity finding),
  tags **merged across all blocks**, **highest severity wins**, internal whitespace folded to `-` — and
  **falls back** to #2797's keyword classifier when untagged (silent-merge still prevented because #2797
  prove-its every REJECT keyword-independently). Wired as the critic's `deps.classifyFlaw`.
- Added to the #2806 fleet-dev manifest (member + `// tier: 3`).
