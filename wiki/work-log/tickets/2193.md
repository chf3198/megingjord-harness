## Research questions (initial; will be refined across iterations)

1. **Gate placement** — where do enforcement checks live? CI workflow (post-PR-open)? Pretool hook (pre-fleet-call)? Both? What's the failure UX for legitimate exceptions?
2. **Tier-vocabulary validation** — how to programmatically reject `wrapProviderCall('ollama', cb, {tier: <wrong>})` without breaking legitimate diagnostic/test cases?
3. **Comment-parsing contract** — what schema must fleet-review guest-comments emit? How strict? Classification proportions enforced or recommended?
4. **Cross-runtime enforcement parity** — how do Codex + Copilot teams integrate without forking the enforcement layer? TEAM_QUESTION sign-off contract per `instructions/cross-team-artifact-write.instructions.md`
5. **Exception/override mechanism** — when MUST fleet-review be bypassable? (e.g., fleet host offline; lane:trivial PRs). Per `feedback-bypass-env-counter` memory, bypass env vars trigger self-anneal at 2+ uses
6. **Hook intercept design** — pretool hook for `curl` to fleet host — what's the redirect message? How avoid canonical-main-enforcer-style overreach (#2107 false-positive history)
7. **Cost-floor protection** — does the enforcement add agent-cycle overhead beyond Phase-0 #2174's budget (<3K incremental tokens/cycle, <2 min added clock)?
8. **Goal-lens override audit** — any enforcement gate that overrides G3/G7 (cost/throughput) for G1 (governance) must cite evidence per `instructions/global-standards.instructions.md` goal-lens lint
9. **Multi-iteration learning** — what new failure-mode classes does iteration N+1 surface that iteration N missed? (forces the iterative loop to produce signal, not just noise)
