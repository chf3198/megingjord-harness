## Objective
Phase-1b of Path D. Wire the megalint `merge-evidence` rule into two callsites:

1. **Live workflow** — fires on `issues.closed`. Fetches merged PR refs for the closing issue, calls the rule, posts advisory comment + applies `governance:close-without-merge` label on violation.
2. **Daily reconciler** — cron-scheduled. Scans recently-closed issues, batches the rule call, posts advisory + labels offenders. Backfills existing closed-without-merge offenders so future-only changes don't strand history.

Both advisory-only (no enforcement) — soak data flows into Phase-1c promotion decision.
