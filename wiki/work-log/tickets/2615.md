## Body

## Objective
Create and drive a self-anneal Epic to eliminate workflow stoppages caused by baton-order friction, stale gate reads, closeout/push deadlocks, and wait-for-green workflow breaks.

## Problem
Recent governed sessions have stalled or ended early because workflow gates and baton sequencing can create deadlock-like operator states:
- merge blocked while required checks are pending, but the session exits instead of remaining in wait-for-green mode
- push blocked by closeout-preflight ordering and stale/late artifact visibility
- baton sequencing pressure can force consultant-style evidence before normal PR progression
- recurring workflow friction is not converted quickly enough into a structured self-anneal lane
