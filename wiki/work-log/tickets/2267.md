## Body

Refs Epic #2258

Problem:
A new governance lane is likely to over-accept or over-reject unless it is replayed against representative historical cases.

Objective:
Add regression and replay coverage for valid and invalid no-code remediation scenarios.

Acceptance criteria:
- Include valid already-shipped ticket-drift normalization cases.
- Include invalid cases where code/admin activity should force the normal baton.
- Include false-positive cases such as stale advisory cleanup on already-merged tickets.
- Provide evidence that validator behavior is stable across the replay corpus.
