## Objective
Implement search-tool routing policy to select free/fleet paths first, then governed Tavily usage, with deterministic fallback to alternatives.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Router decision order is explicitly encoded and tested.
- Free-first selection is default for eligible workloads.
- Tavily selection requires policy-allowed conditions and budget headroom.
- Fallback to alternate provider executes when Tavily is unavailable or blocked by policy.
- Route decisions emit observability fields for auditability.
