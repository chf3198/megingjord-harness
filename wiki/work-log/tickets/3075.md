## Body

Phase-1 child of #3069 (Refs #3069). Phase-0 source: #3070.

## Scope (consideration 4)
Give the loop memory without giving any single reviewer in-family coaching.

## Acceptance criteria
- AC1: a per-Epic review ledger records round, design-hash, each critique, and resolution-plus-evidence; appended to each round's reviewer context.
- AC2: reviewers evaluate the DELTA and must not re-raise a critique already marked resolved-with-evidence in the ledger (regression: the rebutted Makefiles critique does not recur).
- AC3: reviewer IDENTITY stays fresh per dispatch (no cross-call session reuse) so the cross-family no-coaching invariant holds; the ledger is data, not a persisted reviewer.
- AC4: stress test with N rounds asserts ledger growth is bounded and idempotent.
