## Root cause
- New code authored from scratch often has single-letter vars and longer-than-30-line functions before refactor pass.
- Local pre-push readability scan and CI readability scan have different file-path coverage (different counts: local ~253 files, CI ~263 files). This means local pass does NOT guarantee CI pass.

## Proposed remediation
- A1: Align local pre-push scan with CI scan-path config (single source of truth).
- A2: Make readability lint emit suggestions inline during edit (real-time, like ESLint), not just at push time.
- A3: Consider per-file readability budget delta tracking, not absolute count threshold.
