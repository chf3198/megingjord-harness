## Goal
Use current web research to pressure-test the proposed review-score and self-anneal approach against modern evaluation, calibration, governance, and escalation practices.

## Questions
- How do modern scoring rubrics avoid false precision when a single numeric score is used for human and machine review?
- What calibration or guardrail patterns are used when numeric scores drive escalation decisions?
- What evidence, explanation, and audit trails are recommended when scores trigger operational follow-up?
- What thresholds or banding approaches are used in adjacent governance systems?

## Deliverable
A research memo with web-sourced findings and a recommendation on whether the proposed 1-100 / F / B+ / A+ framing should be adopted, adjusted, or split into separate workflows.
