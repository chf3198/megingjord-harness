## Problem
The harness has strong governance, review, and self-anneal mechanisms, but the review lifecycle is still fragmented across teams and roles. The current proposal is to evaluate whether every review should receive a canonical 1-100 score against the harness goals, then map the score to self-anneal routing and escalation thresholds.

## Proposal to evaluate
- Reviews score on a 1-100 scale against the harness goals.
- Scores below 70 are treated as F / broken and require P1 self-annealing.
- Scores below B+ require P2 self-annealing.
- Scores below A+ require at least further analysis before any self-anneal decision.

## Goal
Define and, if validated, implement a consistent review-governance contract that works across teams and roles, produces comparable scores, and routes self-anneal follow-up work predictably.

## Scope
This epic is the parent for research, design, implementation, and validation work needed to decide whether the proposed rubric should become the standard review and self-anneal mechanism.
