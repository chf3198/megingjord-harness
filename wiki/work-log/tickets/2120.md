## Body

## Problem

`CHANGELOG.md` currently produces 89 markdown-lint errors (88× MD024 duplicate-heading + 1× MD001 heading-increment), causing the `lint-required` CI gate to fail on every open PR. Errors were introduced by the #2090 changelog-aggregation merge (commit dbb2ac4) which prepended `.changes/unreleased/*.md` fragments without normalizing heading hierarchy or de-duplicating section names within a release.

## Why an Epic (not a quick patch)

The choice between a one-line MD024 config bypass vs a structural aggregator fix is non-trivial. The trade-offs touch:

- **Keep-a-Changelog standard compliance** (community/industry convention)
- **Aggregator design** (per-fragment vs per-release normalization; conflict prevention; release-tag boundary handling)
- **CI gate strictness vs operator velocity** (cost of false blockers vs cost of silent regressions)
- **Downstream consumer tooling** (changelog renderers, release notes generators, GitHub Releases auto-parsing)

A naive patch (`"MD024": false`) silences the rule but loses the duplicate-detection signal everywhere it's legitimately useful (within a single release section, accidentally repeated sub-headings). A heavy restructure costs hours and may not match best-practice format that downstream tools expect.
