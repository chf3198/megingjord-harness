## Body

The current `config/doc-coverage-matrix.yml` covers 9 area labels but leaves significant documentation gaps: root README.md is not required for most areas, ARCHITECTURE.md is absent, community health files (SECURITY.md, CONTRIBUTING.md) are unmapped, and per-area docs (skills indexes, agent directories, copilot-instructions, CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md) are missing.

## Problem
Tickets can ship without any governance prompt to update: ARCHITECTURE.md, CONTRIBUTING.md, SECURITY.md, docs/skills-copilot.md, docs/skills-agents.md, .github/copilot-instructions.md, CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md, governance/README.md, docs/howto/baton-workflow.md.

## Solution
Expand every area entry in the matrix with all relevant required + suggested surfaces. Expand area:infra to require ARCHITECTURE.md. Expand area:instructions to require runtime adapter files. Expand area:governance to require governance/README.md and baton-workflow.md.
