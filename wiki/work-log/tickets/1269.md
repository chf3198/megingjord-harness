## Per role-baton-routing.instructions.md

`status:backlog` should NOT carry `role:*` labels (except Epics, which carry `role:manager`). The proper transitions:

- backlog → triage: Manager picks up, applies role:manager
- triage → ready: MANAGER_HANDOFF emitted; role label removed
- ready → in-progress: Collaborator picks up, applies role:collaborator

So `status:backlog + role:collaborator` skips the triage/ready transition and is governance-incorrect.

## Suggested fix (per affected team)

Either:
1. **Move ticket to status:in-progress** (if work has actually started; role:collaborator is then appropriate), OR
2. **Remove role:collaborator label** (if ticket is still queued; role joins back when Collaborator picks up)
