## Goal

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AC1: `docs/howto/hamr-rotation-validator-architecture.md` describes the validator location: as a HAMR-side `/mcp` capability OR as a separate worker route.

- [ ] AC6: Integration with `/quota` — rotation-violation events surface in the quota stale check.
- [ ] AC7: Cross-references Phase 1.1 inventory output to confirm the validator can satisfy the rules with HAMR-routed providers.
