## Task: Expand G4 Privacy → Privacy & Security

**Epic:** #1526
**Depends on:** #1527 (research)

### What
Rename G4 from "Privacy" to "Privacy & Security" and expand its definition to explicitly cover:
- Sensitive context stays local unless explicit override (existing)
- Secrets never in git history, env vars, or logs
- Least-privilege tokens (GITHUB_TOKEN scopes, Actions permissions)
- Supply-chain pinning for Actions and dependencies
- Injection threat controls for agent-consumed inputs

G11 (standalone Security goal) is **not added** — these controls belong under G4.

### Why
Privacy and Security share the same trust boundary. Separating them creates definitional overlap and enforcement gaps. The Phase 2 research (supply-chain, least-privilege, arXiv alignment faking) reinforces that security controls must be co-located with privacy controls in the goal hierarchy.
