## Objective
Phase-1c of Path D. Promote merge-evidence from advisory to **required** at PR-merge time. Add a megalint validator that fails PRs whose body does not commit to closing the linked non-lightweight issue on merge (via `Closes #N` / `Fixes #N` / `Resolves #N` auto-close trigger). Operator override via the issue-level label `merge-evidence-override:approved` (mirrors the `[skip-changelog]` pattern from `release-docs-hygiene.instructions.md`).

This closes the audit-identified gap: tickets reaching `status:done` while no commit referencing them lands on main. With this gate, every code-change-lane PR commits to atomically closing its issue on merge OR explicitly opts out.
