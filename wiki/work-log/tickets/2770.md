Phase-1 of Epic #2291; consumes Phase-0 design #2292 (D7 step 2). Closes the G9 cross-runtime gap: the canonical shim is JS-only; 16 Python hooks have no `.env` path.

## Scope
Add `hooks/scripts/load_local_env.py` mirroring the JS contract: fill-don't-override, names-only redaction, graceful on missing/malformed, `MEGINGJORD_NO_DOTENV=1` opt-out, `MEGINGJORD_DOTENV_PATH` relocate. Wire the 16 credential-consuming Python hooks.

## AC
- AC1: `load_local_env.py` passes a pytest suite asserting all 5 contract semantics.
- AC2: the 16 hooks hydrate before reading `os.environ` keys.
- AC3: exact redaction/graceful parity with the JS shim.

test_strategy: tdd-pyramid (pytest).
