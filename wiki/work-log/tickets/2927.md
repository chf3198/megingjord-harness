## Problem
The Red-Team Reviewer agent (.claude/agents/red-team.md) declares `model: claude-sonnet-4-6`. The agent's entire job is to call `node scripts/global/fleet-red-team-dispatch.js` — it dispatches to a fleet Qwen model and formats output.
