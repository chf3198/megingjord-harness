## Goal

Investigate when crypto-signing of Team&Model becomes mandatory and what fingerprinting techniques exist for operators who do not adopt crypto-signing.

## Research questions

1. What's the minimum crypto-signing infrastructure needed (key generation, distribution, rotation, revocation)? Existing `Crypto-Algorithm: ed25519`, `Crypto-Key-Id`, `Crypto-Signature` fields are already optional in baton artifacts.
2. For non-crypto operators, what fingerprinting techniques can detect spoofed signatures? Candidates: output-style perplexity profiling; response-pattern correlation (consistent token-distribution signatures per model); response-time profiling.
3. What is the trust model? Open-source operators may not be able to maintain signing keys; what trade-off keeps the contract enforceable while preserving G5 portability?
4. Compare to existing literature: TEP (Trusted Envelope Protocol), MJW Media's cryptographic LLM agent security layer, CMU SEI chain-of-custody patterns.
