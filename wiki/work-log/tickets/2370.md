## Root cause hypothesis

The cross-team-artifact-write contract in `instructions/cross-team-artifact-write.instructions.md` requires TEAM_RESPONSE artifacts from target teams before MANAGER_HANDOFF advances. Today the contract has NO mechanism preventing the source team from FABRICATING the response artifacts and self-advancing the baton. The PGP web-of-trust principle applies: you cannot sign for someone else; they must sign for themselves. The contract is enforceable only if the TEAM_RESPONSE artifact carries a cryptographic signature from a target-team key, OR if the artifact originates from the target-team's GitHub identity (currently all teams share the `chf3198` identity via different runtimes).
