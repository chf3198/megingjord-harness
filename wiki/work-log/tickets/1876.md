### Acceptance Criteria

**AC 1: Linter Rule â€” Epic Workflow**
- Detect when Epic carries `role:collaborator` or `role:admin` (invalid)
- Detect when Epic lacks `role:consultant` during `status:review` (mandatory)
- Report: "Epic must not carry role:collaborator or role:admin. Epic requires role:consultant during status:review phase."
- Exclude: Terminal states (status:done, status:cancelled)

**AC 2: Linter Rule â€” Child Ticket Workflow**
- Detect when child ticket skips from `status:testing` â†’ `status:done` without `status:review`

**AC 3: CI Gate Integration**
- `.github/workflows/baton-gates.yml` must invoke linter before merge
- Enforce: No PRs merge if workflow drift detected on linked tickets
- Block message: "Governance linter: role-baton workflow violation on [#N]. Fix before merge."

**AC 4: Local Pre-Commit Gate**
- `scripts/global/role-baton-linter.js` validates locally (can be run by any role)

**AC 5: Audit Report**
