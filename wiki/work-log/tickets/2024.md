## Body

## Problem
As observed in the recent Epic #1956 lifecycles, there is a risk of ticket governance drift (e.g. parent Epics remaining stuck in backlog status while all children are closed, prose-only child references bypassing sub-issue validations, and progress logging omissions). To maintain long-term compliance across multiple teams and model fleets, we need an impassible, automated governance guardrail.

## Goal
Design, research, and establish a permanent, automated guardrail that completely prevents future ticket governance and epic lifecycle drift (e.g. auto-linking, status synchronizations, and validation checks).
