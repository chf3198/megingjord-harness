# #2912 — Promote cross-team response fidelity and TEAM_QUESTION sign-off to hard blocking gates

## Body

## Problem
Cross-team response fidelity (Rule 2.5 from the guardrail assessment) is advisory-only after one soak cycle. The `scripts/global/megalint/cross-team-response-fidelity.js` validator emits an advisory when a TEAM_RESPONSE signer's team doesn't match the `from:` field. The gate does not block merges.

This means a source team can post a TEAM_RESPONSE on behalf of the target team without the gate blocking.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Promote `cross-team-response-fidelity.js` from advisory to hard-blocking (remove `continue-on-error: true` in baton-gates.yml)
- [ ] Add TEAM_QUESTION sign-off gate: if `target_team_sign_off: pending` in MANAGER_HANDOFF at time of merge, block merge
- [ ] Add regression test: TEAM_RESPONSE from wrong team → gate fails
- [ ] Increment soak counter; soak period is complete
