- AC2: **routing guard** — a fleet-dev lane is offered only when `module-installed/enabled ∧

- AC5: tests — fleetless (MINIMUM_TIER<3 OR module absent) ⇒ harness == baseline (no fleet lane, no
  error); module-present ∧ Tier-3 ⇒ fleet-dev available. Stress: install/uninstall lifecycle.
