## Problem

Claude Code and Copilot have carried much of the recent harness implementation. We need an independent Codex Team critical analysis to ensure the Megingjord harness remains fully compatible with OpenAI Codex workflows and does not drift toward Anthropic/Copilot-specific assumptions.

## Scope

Perform a Codex-centered audit of harness goals, features, workflows, runtime assets, and governance assumptions. Identify compatibility strengths, gaps, risks, and concrete recommendations.
