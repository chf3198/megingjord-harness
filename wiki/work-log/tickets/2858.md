## Body

## Problem (Anneal trigger)

During the Epic #2827 red-team research loop, `fleet-red-team-dispatch.js` was invoked as a CLI but exited immediately without output — because it exports functions but has no CLI `main()` entry point. The correct dispatcher is `cascade-dispatch.js --prompt ... --model qwen2.5-coder:32b`.

Additional finding: `cascade-dispatch.js` routes through LiteLLM at `http://<IPV4_REDACTED>:4000` (OpenClaw). When LiteLLM returns an empty/slow response, the cascade silently escalates to free-cloud (Gemini) without emitting a stderr signal, making the tier visible only via `--json` inspection.

## Anneal Decision
`file-ticket` — structural/repeatable gap affecting every future red-team loop invocation.

## Research Questions
- R1: Should `fleet-red-team-dispatch.js` grow a CLI entry point (wrapping `dispatchRedTeam`)? Or should the docs/skills explicitly name `cascade-dispatch.js` as the correct CLI path?
- R2: How should the cascade log fleet→free-cloud tier escalation to stderr so operators see it without `--json`?
- R3: Is the LiteLLM proxy at OpenClaw the intended fleet routing path, or should HAMR route directly to Ollama at `<IPV4_REDACTED>:11434` for red-team calls?
