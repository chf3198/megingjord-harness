## Body

## Objective

Phase-0 research for Epic #1112 AC-R4. Calibrate the right termination cap for cross-team R&D synthesis runs of varying complexity. #1105 closed in 6.5h vs the 72h cap; the gap suggests either the cap was over-budgeted or the synthesis was unusually efficient.

## Research questions

- Distribution of synthesis-run wall-clock from empirical data (one run so far: #1105)
- Predictive model: can run-duration be estimated from Epic size (children count + AC count + research-vs-implementation split)?
- Stability-detection termination (NeurIPS 2025 Kolmogorov-Smirnov) vs hard cap: when does adaptive termination outperform fixed cap?
- Cost of being wrong: under-cap risks premature termination; over-cap risks zombie runs. Which goal-lens (G3 cost vs G6 resilience) wins?

## Deliverable

Research markdown at  covering:
- Empirical baseline
- Three termination-strategy proposals (fixed-cap, adaptive-only, hybrid)
- Recommendation with cost-of-error analysis
