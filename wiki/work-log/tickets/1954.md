## Body

## Problem

The Megingjord Harness portability architecture is not visible from any external-facing doc surface. A new user, contributor, or developer reading the README, ARCHITECTURE.md, or docs/howto/ cannot immediately answer:

- What gets installed globally vs. per-workspace?
- Do I have to set up each project separately?
- Are Copilot, Claude Code, and Codex equally supported?

## Objective

Make the two-tier model (global `~/.copilot/`/`~/.claude/`/`~/.codex/` + per-workspace adapter files) and multi-runtime parity (all three runtimes are first-class) obvious from the project's primary documentation surfaces.
