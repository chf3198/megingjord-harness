## Context
Part of Epic #1626. Independent.

`scripts/wiki/eval-harness.js` currently reports `mean_precision=0.12, mean_recall=0.50, gate=FAIL`. The quality floor is 0.40. Two root causes:
1. Two of five expected corpus slugs are missing: `fleet-portable-config` and `anthropic-batch-routing`
2. BM25 ranking in `scripts/wiki/retrieval.js` does not weight title matches higher than body matches, so exact-slug queries rank the target page low

## Objective
- Add the 2 missing stub pages to the corpus
- Add title-boost scoring to `retrieval.js` (title match ≈ 2× body match weight in BM25)  
- Maintain and expand `eval-ground-truth.json` to cover ≥ 8 queries (enough to validate quality at scale)
- Pass the eval gate: `mean_precision ≥ 0.40`

## Implementation notes

- After adding pages and title boost, re-run `node scripts/wiki/eval-harness.js` to confirm gate passes
