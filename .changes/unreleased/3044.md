## [#3044] Copilot BYOK signer aliases registry-derivable

**Added** explicit `aliasSeed` registry entries in `inventory/team-model-signatures.json`
for Copilot BYOK model families that previously fell through to the generic "Nova" default:

- `copilot:gemini-*` → **Gaia** (e.g. gemini-2.5-flash, gemini-2.5-pro)
- `copilot:gpt-4*` → **Gale** (e.g. gpt-4.1, gpt-4o)
- `copilot:o[0-9]*` → **Onyx** (e.g. o1, o3)

`node scripts/global/agent-signature.js --team copilot --model gemini-2.5-flash --role collaborator`
now yields `Gaia Harper` instead of `Nova Harper`, making admin-gate `analyzeComments`
accept Copilot BYOK baton artifacts that previously parked tickets indefinitely (#2945 / Epic #3021).

**Added** `tests/copilot-byok-signer.spec.js` — 17 regression assertions covering all
three new families plus non-regression of existing Copilot aliases (Soren/Orion/Milo/Coda).
