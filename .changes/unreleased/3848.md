### P1-b: per-repo override resolver + G8 audit (Epic #2892)

Adds `scripts/global/override-resolver.js`: resolves a repo-local `.megingjord/overrides.{yml,json}`,
applies the P1-a hard-floor (Tier-H security/provenance keys are rejected, never resolved), returns the
effective Tier-A/C override map, enumerates a repo's active overrides (`activeOverrides`, for G8/census),
and emits a redacted schema-v3 `override-applied` event per active override (`auditOverrides`). Net-positive
observability; no new bypass surface and no duplicate hard-floor authority (reuses P1-a).
