### Fixed
- Session blast-radius push counter (G-15, Refs #2913) now counts only genuine, successful code-shipping pushes. Previously any `git push`-shaped command incremented the counter in the PostToolUse hook regardless of intent or outcome, so a routine branch-delete (`git push --delete`) or a push rejected by the pre-push gate drifted `push_count` above the static `pushes_in_session` limit and produced a false oversight halt on an otherwise-clean ship (e.g. `total_pushes=6 > pushes_in_session=5`). A new pure predicate `admin_patterns.is_countable_push(cmd, tool_response)` excludes branch-deletes, dry-runs, and rejected pushes (non-zero exit or push-rejection markers), while genuine off-the-books pushes still count and unknown outcomes count (safety-preserving). Covered by `tests/hooks/test_push_counter_true_ships.py`. (#3265)
