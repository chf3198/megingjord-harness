### Security
- Promote collaborator self-check from advisory-only to hard pre-PR gate (#2907): `collaborator-self-check.js` now exits 1 on failure, `megalint/collaborator-handoff.js` blocks when the "Pre-handoff verification" block is absent or FAIL, `lefthook.yml` pre-push includes the gate, and a new required CI workflow (`collaborator-self-check-required.yml`) replaces the advisory workflow. Opt-out: `collaborator-self-check:waived` label or `COLLABORATOR_SELF_CHECK_SKIP=1` env var (emits advisory warning). Lightweight lanes (trivial, docs-research, config-only, no-code-remediation) are excluded. Refs OWASP ASI09.
