### Added
- Epic task-list auto-tick workflow: when a child issue closes, the workflow scans open Epics for typed child-ref task-list lines (`- [ ] #<closed-N> <sep>`) and atomically flips them to `- [x]`. Prose mentions of other ticket numbers in unrelated AC bullets are not affected. Handles prose-style linkage only; Sub-issues primitive uses native GitHub auto-rollup. New: `scripts/global/epic-task-list-auto-tick.js`, `.github/workflows/epic-task-list-auto-tick.yml`, `tests/epic-task-list-auto-tick.spec.js`. Refs #1337.
