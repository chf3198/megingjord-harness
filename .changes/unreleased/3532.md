### Changed
- **Signer-independence is no longer self-satisfiable via role-persona splitting (#3532).**
  The Admin-merge independence check now decides on the `Team&Model` **TEAM segment** (not the
  persona surname): `ADMIN_HANDOFF` and `COLLABORATOR_HANDOFF` must sign under genuinely
  different teams, **OR** the `ADMIN_HANDOFF` must cite a **verified cross-family consensus
  receipt**. A single agent that signs all four baton roles under one `Team&Model` (the
  #3518/#3521 loophole) now **fails** the gate. Enforced across `baton-independence.js`,
  `baton-authority/evidence-loader.js` (the `SIGNER_INDEPENDENT` bit), `merge-authority.js`,
  and the megalint `admin-handoff` validator.

### Added
- **Un-forgeable cross-family consensus receipt** (`scripts/global/cross-family-receipt.js`,
  `cross-family-consensus.js`). A `$0` panel of ≥2 model families distinct from the authoring
  family (each able to REJECT) is dispatched via the free-cloud fleet; their **actual responses**
  are logged to an append-only, **hash-chained** committed ledger
  (`governance/cross-family-consensus.jsonl`) and the receipt is `sha256` over them. A new CI
  validator (`baton-authority/consensus-receipt-check.js`, wired into `baton-authority-merge.yml`)
  re-verifies the receipt from committed evidence — chain integrity, sha256 match, family
  diversity, and unanimous PASS. A genuine cross-family consensus PASS is thereby equivalent to
  independent authorization for the Admin merge. The existing `cross_family_receipt` (16-hex)
  field in `collaborator-handoff.js` is reconciled onto the same shared format/algorithm.
