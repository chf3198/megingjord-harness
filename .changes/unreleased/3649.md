### Changed
- **flaw-emission validator softened** (#3649, Epic #2070): the blocking `closeout-lint`
  flaw-emission gate no longer false-fails on ordinary engineering prose. Weak noun markers
  (`flaw|bug|failure|incident`) are skipped when they form a benign compound (`failure mode`,
  `bug-fix`, `failure patterns`), a structured field key (`flaw=`/`flaw:`), or the
  `incidents.jsonl` citation token; strong first-person confession markers (`I had to`,
  `worked around`, `side-effect`) retain full sensitivity. `CITE` now recognizes the Epic
  #3425 structured-block forms (`decision:`, `artifact:`, `flaws_recognized:`) so self-citing
  flaw blocks pass. Recall on the historical uncited-flaw corpus is preserved (replay-eval).
