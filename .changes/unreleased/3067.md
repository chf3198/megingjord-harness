### Added
- Daily local-first wiki reconciliation + Tier-0 signed archive (#3067, Epic #3063): new `.github/workflows/wiki-reconcile-cron.yml` (07:30 UTC) repairs anything a missed or failed wiki event left stale. `scripts/wiki/reconcile.js` reconciles Wiki A from **local git** (`git show HEAD:path`, no network) and Wiki B against `gh` truth, falling back to a **checksum-validated cache** when `gh` is unreachable. The cache-use ladder never silently no-ops: a fresh cache (under 24h) logs a Tier-1 advisory, a cache over 24h with `gh` unreachable escalates to Tier-2, and both-paths-fail is always Tier-2; a prolonged outage (over 48h) serves the last-known-good static zip. Emits schema-v3 audit events (repaired list, drift delta, `cache_used` distinct from `reconcile_error_rate`) to `dashboard/events.jsonl`.
- `scripts/wiki/archive-snapshot.js` publishes an Ed25519-signed SHA256 manifest over a static `.zip` of Wiki A/B/C to `wiki/archive/static/` (Tier-0), verifiable offline. New `npm run stress:wiki-reconcile`; local fallback `npm run wiki:reindex` documented in `wiki/archive/README.md`.
