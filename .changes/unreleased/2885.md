### Fixed
- Fleet-dev tests no longer pollute production telemetry (#2885). The `defaultEmit` of `fleet-dev-governor.js` (#2796) and `fleet-dev-gates.js` (#2795) wrote real `~/.megingjord/*.jsonl` records even under the test runner, corrupting the #2800 cost report's escalation/demotion data (~9k junk records). Cleaned the polluted files; the cost report now reads clean. Cross-family reviewed (gemini-2.5-pro, ACCEPT 10/10 after a 3-iteration loop that caught a path-traversal regression pre-merge).
