### Fixed

- **Merge-gate deadlock (Epic #3679):** `baton-authority/merge` no longer self-referentially
  deadlocks. The CI_GREEN rollup in `merge-authority.js` now excludes its own reporting
  context(s) (`KNOWN_REPORTING_CONTEXTS`) — previously the gate counted itself (in_progress
  while evaluating), so `CI_GREEN` could never be satisfied from live PR data and clean
  tickets were forced through an `--admin` bypass. (#3699)
