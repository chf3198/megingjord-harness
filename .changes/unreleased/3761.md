- **Wiki retrieval wired into the Consultant pre-critique, with a measured token-cost reduction (#3761, Epic #3719).**
  The shipped lexical retrieval floor is now invoked in a real baton step: `multi-model-critique` grounds its
  cross-family pre-critique on the top-N relevant wiki pages (plaintext, over the fresh wiki-mirror) via the new
  `scripts/wiki/retrieval-baton.js#groundArtifact`, instead of loading the whole store. Measured on the live wiki
  (top-N 5): **99.1% fewer tokens** (170 candidate pages → 5 retrieved; 111,180 → 1,005 tokens). Each grounded
  critique records a schema-v3 G3 `retrieval-token-cost` signal. Grounding is backward-compatible (`ground:false`
  opts out) and degrades to an ungrounded critique on retrieval failure. Cross-refs #2093 (not closed).
