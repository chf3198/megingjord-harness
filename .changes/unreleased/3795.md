### Added

- `scripts/global/canary-emitter.js` (#3795, Epic #3789) ‚Äî post-merge canary + auto-rollback
  signals for the security-surface lane. Emits `event:merged-dark` / `event:canary-start` /
  `event:canary-rollback` / `event:canary-promote` (event-schema-v3) to `dashboard/events.jsonl`,
  with a fixed canary-metrics schema and a `rollbackPredicate` (breach ‚Üí flag-flip rollback in
  seconds, not a revert commit).
- `scripts/global/dark-not-flipped-inventory.js` ‚Äî a standing G8 gauge over lane flags that were
  merged-dark but never flipped (state `dark`/`canary`), making the verify-then-flip gap visible.
- `scripts/global/megalint/flag-lifecycle-lint.js` + `config/flag-lifecycle.json` ‚Äî a CI lint
  (wired into `lint-required`) that **fails** when a dark-launch flag past its `retire_by` is still
  referenced, forcing dead-flag removal or an explicit extension (closes progressive-delivery debt).
- `scripts/global/baton-artifact-schema.js` extended (all fields optional, corpus-safe): a new
  `CANARY_REPORT` artifact, an `ephemeral_verification_evidence` block on `COLLABORATOR_HANDOFF`,
  `flag_name`/`flag_default`/`fail_closed_proof`/`merge_reversibility` on `ADMIN_HANDOFF`, and
  `residual_risk_after_dark`/`flip_decision_owner` on `CONSULTANT_CLOSEOUT`.
- Tests: `tests/canary-emitter.spec.js`, `tests/flag-lifecycle-lint.spec.js` (tdd-pyramid) and
  `tests/stress-canary-emitter.spec.js` (stress-test: 500-way concurrent-append integrity G6 + a
  rollback-predicate p99 budget G7).
