### Changed
- Fleet dispatch request-timeouts are now configuration-driven (#2901). `fleet-red-team-dispatch.js` reads `REQUEST_TIMEOUT_MS` from `config/timeout-policy.json`'s `fleet-red-team-rate` class (1200s, empirically derived at Phase-0 #2174 where qwen2.5-coder:32b hit p99=907s on the Tailscale fleet) and `ollama-direct.js` reads `TIMEOUT_MS` from `fleet-dispatch-basic` (300s), each falling back to the prior hardcoded value (600s / 120s) on a missing or malformed policy. A 30s `unref`'d stderr heartbeat (cleared on completion) now signals progress during slow fleet generations so they aren't mistaken for a hung terminal — preventing premature availability-failure classification that would push work to paid providers (G3). The timeout loaders are exported and path-injectable with unit coverage. Cross-family reviewed (gemini-2.5-pro, ACCEPT 9/10).
