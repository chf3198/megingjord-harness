fix(governance): enforce Phase-0 to Phase-1 promotion gate for research-first Epics (#2680, #2681, #2682, #2683; Epic #2678)

Adds a deterministic `phase0GreenComplete` predicate, auto-materialization of a Phase-1 seed child when a research-first Epic's Phase-0 goes green with zero Phase-1 children, and a closure-block guard that posts a structured `BLOCKER_NOTE`, emits a `phase0-complete-no-phase1` incident, reopens the Epic, and fails the run — closing the #2661 silent-close gap. Runtime-agnostic across Copilot/Codex/Claude Code/Antigravity. `PHASE0_GATE_BYPASS=1` provides an audited advisory escape hatch.
