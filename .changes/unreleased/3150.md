### Added
- `scripts/global/benchmark-gate.js` (#3150, Epic #3147 S2): the continuous-benchmark enforcement gate. `runGate({results, required, present})` (a) **fails on any benchmark metric that regressed beyond budget** vs its golden baseline (seed runs pass), and (b) **flags a required surface lacking a benchmark suite**. Advisory by default; `--strict` opts into a non-zero exit; advisory→blocking promotion is replay-eval-gated (no calendar). Deterministic, local, $0. Ships with a **stress spec** (`tests/stress-benchmark-gate.spec.js`: a fault-path G6 + a p99 perf-budget G7) — dogfooding the Epic's own rule that a `*-gate.js` surface gets a stress suite.
