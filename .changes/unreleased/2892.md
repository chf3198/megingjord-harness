### Phase-0 research: per-repo guardrail override contract (#2892)

Delivered the Phase-0 synthesis for per-repo guardrail overrides. Key finding: the "no override mechanism
exists" premise had drifted — a de-facto sprawl of ~40+ env flags / bypass toggles / config surfaces
already exists with no unified contract or explicit hard-floor. The synthesis inventories + classifies
them into three tiers — **hard-floor (never overridable — security/provenance controls)**,
overridable-with-audit, and freely-configurable — and proposes a consolidated, audited (G8) per-repo
override contract that is net-positive (it *defends* the security-weakening carve-out, adds no new
weakening surface). Validated by a ≥90 cross-family council (mistral 98 / meta 96 / deepseek 98; Gwet 1.0).
