- **Optional local/free embedding acceleration for wiki retrieval (#3762, Epic #3719).** A new flag-gated
  (`WIKI_EMBEDDINGS_ENABLED`, default OFF) embedding-ranked retrieval path (`scripts/wiki/embedding-retrieval.js`)
  sits over the mandatory lexical floor: when enabled and the local loopback-only Ollama embedder
  (`nomic-embed-text`, zero-cost) is reachable it ranks pages by cosine similarity; when the flag is off or the
  embedder is absent it degrades gracefully to the shipped lexical `hybridSearch` (`mode` flagged). Promotion to
  default is gated on a precision/recall replay-eval beating the lexical baseline + clearing the corpus quality
  floor — never a calendar (ship advisory; promotion deferred). No hosted/paid vector DB; air-gapped operators are
  unaffected.
