### Added

- Two governance-gate validators (Epic #2517): `scripts/global/megalint/admin-merge-exception.js` (AC1 — an admin-bypass merge must carry a `merge-bypass:admin-exception` label OR a `BLOCKER_NOTE` with `bypass_reason:`+`approver:`) and `scripts/global/megalint/batch-cancel-evidence.js` (AC2 — a multi-close PR where any closing issue is cancelled requires a per-issue `CANCELLATION: <reason>` comment). Both registered in megalint; `.github/workflows/merge-bypass-gates.yml` runs them post-merge (fail-on-violation). Promotion to a required branch-protection check is a documented sign-off-gated step.
